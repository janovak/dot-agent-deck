- **Named Workspaces (`--workspace <name>`)**
  Save and reload multiple distinct pane layouts under names of your choice — useful when you keep separate sets of sessions for different projects or clients. Launch with `dot-agent-deck --workspace <name>` to load (or start fresh in) a named workspace; on exit, whatever panes are open are auto-saved back to that workspace's file. The existing `--continue` flag is unchanged and still drives the unnamed default slot. `dot-agent-deck workspaces list` and `dot-agent-deck workspaces delete <name>` round out the housekeeping. Workspace files live at `~/.config/dot-agent-deck/workspaces/<name>.toml`; names are restricted to `[A-Za-z0-9_-]{1,64}` and Windows reserved device names are rejected.
